Campus Staff And Faculty

Why Work for the CSU?

As the nation’s largest and most diverse university system, the California State University seeks faculty and staff who reflect our diversity and
commitment to inclusion. Faculty who join one of our 23 campuses will work with gifted and recognized colleagues, and all positions at the CSU
offer competitive compensation and benefits, a supportive working environment and the chance to advance profession​ally.
