CSU IS THE MOST CONSEQUENTIAL PUBLIC UNIVERSITY SYSTEM IN THE NATION

We are the nation's most diverse four-year university—ethnically, economically and
academically. We transform individual lives and drive California’s economy. Our students
graduate with less (or no) debt and more job-ready skills, empowered to thrive individually
and to contribute mightily to the success of their communities and state.
